About Me

My name is Tomi Novak, I am (well.. I was, I'm currently doing a Computing degree) a web developer with a particular focus on back-end technologies. Most of my professional experience is with the LAMP stack, creating and maintaining content driven websites using different solutions ranging from Perch, WordPess to besopke and proprietary content management systems.
